Types of Cases Tenant Lawyers Near Me Handle

Eviction Defense


Tenant lawyers can defend renters facing wrongful evictions, such as those initiated without proper notice or due to landlord retaliation. They may help tenants stay in their homes by challenging the eviction in court.


Lease Violation Disputes


These landlord tenant attorneys address cases where landlords breach lease terms, such as unauthorized rent increases or failure to provide essential services like heat or water. They negotiate with landlords or take legal action to enforce lease agreements.


Security Deposit Disputes


Tenant attorneys near me can assist in recovering wrongfully withheld security deposits, often due to unjustified deductions for damages. They help tenants gather evidence and pursue legal remedies if necessary.


Habitability Issues

These landlord tenant lawyers advocate for tenants living in unsafe or uninhabitable conditions, such as pest infestations or structural hazards. They may compel landlords to make repairs or seek compensation for tenants affected by neglect.
